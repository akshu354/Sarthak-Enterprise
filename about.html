About

Firozabad Glass Industry

Firozabad, known as the "City of Glass", is famous for its exquisite glass products and traditional craftsmanship. The city has been at the heart of India's glass industry for over 200 years, producing a wide variety of items such as bangles, decorative pieces, and high-quality utility glassware. The artisans here pass down their skills through generations, creating both functional and artistic glass items admired across the globe.

Sarthak Enterprise – A Trusted Name in Glassware

Sarthak Enterprise is a premier glassware showroom offering an elegant and diverse collection of crockery, drinking glasses, and exclusive gift packs. Known for quality and style, Sarthak Enterprise caters to both individual and corporate customers looking for functional yet visually appealing glassware solutions.

As a proud extension of Eagle Glass Industry, a company rooted in excellence since 1992, Sarthak Enterprise inherits a rich legacy of craftsmanship and trust. Eagle Glass Industry was founded by four visionary brothers — Sandeep Jain, Deepak Jain, Pankaj Jain, and Dheeraj Jain, and is strongly supported by Devendra Kumar Jain, whose guidance continues to inspire the enterprise’s values and growth.

With a strong heritage and commitment to quality, Sarthak Enterprise continues to build on its parent company’s vision — delivering premium glassware that brings elegance to every table.
